Are You Being Scammed?

Internet Scammers are everywhere, and they are smart. And since the internet is global, internet scamming is a global problem. They are looking for adresses, bank accounts, credic card information, and they might get it if you aren't careful.

So, Who Gets Scammed?

Scammers are crafty; they'll lie, cheat, and steal to get what they want. They send out mass e-mails, pretending to be a large retailer, trying to access your private information. Some might call your phone, claiming to be the IRS, telling you that they can erase your debt, and all they need is your credit card number. Some scammers even go as far as contacting you about an "Urgent Giveaway" that you would hate to miss. Regardless of what form they take, you'll likely see scams pretty often on the internet.
